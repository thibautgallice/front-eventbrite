Description

FASHION WEEK HAUTE COUTURE SHOW LAFASHIONPARISIENNE PARIS

CECI N'EST PAS UN BILLET / INSCRIVEZ VOUS POUR RECEVOIR LES INFORMATIONS BOOKING

Hi Fashion lovers !

Are you looking to discover Fashion Week shows this 2020 season ? You don't have the contacts to get invitations ?Seeing a real fashion week tour is your dream? no worries ,we are here for you ! You will be able to participe to a fashion show with our semester stylist.

If you are available during this January fashion week , add some spice to your week by going to a Fashion Week show ! This Fashion week moment will be the occasion to make your dreams come true and witness a real fashion show to start 2020 ! The ticket are highly limited since the fashion show are prestigious and unique .

Please pre-book your ticket and we shall send you the informations by email to finalize the booking.This can't be used as the ticket , we are not the organizator of the show

Date et heure

dim., 1 mars 2020, 14:00 –
mar., 3 mars 2020, 15:00 heure : France
